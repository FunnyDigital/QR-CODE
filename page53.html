MARC RAYNER

VICTORY

ACRYLIC AND COLLAGE

2021

5ft x 4ft

Marc is a Swedish sociopolitical artist who uses history, futurism, and a broad spectrum of the human experience to illustrate the concerns in a rapidly changing world. He approaches each painting with the knowledge that the viewer will interpret the work in their unique way.
He will be showing his iconic art pieces for the first time in Africa here at Gallery 2305 Lagos in the spring of 2023.
